A World of Homemade Elegance

by Chef Petrova

We are combining art, imagination and food to create an alternative reality which will wake up all your senses.

This homemade experience is designed to explore even the unthinkable combination of flavours in order to provide an unfogetable experiense filled up with the explorer's adrenaline. And all this is happening whilst one is endulging on a glass of Pinot Noir by the fireplace...

Here we take you on an unforgetable journey across the world from the comfort of your home.

Chef Petrova

Creative, passionate, epicurean, skilful and rigorous, Chef Petrova has the leitmotif of the satisfaction of her clients

Years of experience and continuous drive for excellence have instilled in this talented and singular chef, the taste of rigor and excellence

Its tasty and sharp cuisine respects the seasons and products.

"No deconstruction in my kitchen, I wish that the customer finds the form and taste of the product. A return to basics but with current cooking techniques and contemporary dressage. Tradition and Modernity, it's not incompatible!"
