Chef Petrova

My name is Petya Petrova. I am a self-taught chef born in Bulgaria. I grew up under the kitchen table at my grandmother’s knees. That is where appetite begins for me. Inspiration comes from the world around me – from art, music, slow food and fast cars. Catch the flash in the dark because it only passes once. Expect the unexpected.
