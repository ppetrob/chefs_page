Starters

Kicking off the excitement, here is chef's warm welcome to the table, openning of the show yet to reveal its beauty

    Ingedients

  • 12 king prawns raw, withs heads on
  • 3 tbsp of chilli sauce Sriracha
  • 1 tbsp of chilli flakes red (optional)
  • 3 mangoes
  • 1 tbsp of fresh ginger grated
  • 1 red onion finely chopped
  • 2 limes juiced
  • 2 to matoes deseeded and finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp of coriander finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp of pomegranate seeds
  • sea salt
  • black pepper
Sriracha King Prawns and Mango Salsa
-

Method

  1. Start with the prawns. Bring a saucepan of lightly salted water to the boil. Blanch the prawns for 30 seconds, drain and cool in iced water. Remove the heads, peel and devein, and place them on a chopping board
  2. Brush with Sriracha sauce and season with black pepper and extra chilli flakes (optional)
  3. Heat a large griddle pan over a medium-high heat. Grill the prawns for 1 minute on each side, then remove from the pan and allow to cool slightly
  4. For mango salsa: Peel and finely dice the mango, place in a bowl and mix with the red onion, pomegranate, tomatoes, ginger, lime juice and coriander, and season to taste with salt and pepper
  5. Place a metal pastry ring on a serving plate and fill it with mango salsa. Top with grilled shriracha prawns, sprinkle with lemon zest and chopped coriander and serve immediately
